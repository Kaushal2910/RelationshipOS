# RelationOS Asset Prompts & Guidelines

## Theme & Aesthetic
- **Concept:** Minimalist connection, node networks, intersecting paths, and clean UI geometry.
- **Aesthetic:** Cool, subtle, premium, and abstract. Avoid hearts, roses, or overtly romantic clichés.
- **Colors:** Dominant soft cream/rose white background (`#FDF8F6`), paired with muted charcoal (`#1E1E24`) and terracotta/warm rust (`#E07A5F`) accents.

---

## 1. App Icon (`icon.png`)
- **File Path:** `assets/icon.png`
- **Output:** 1024 × 1024 px, PNG (solid background, no alpha channel transparency for iOS)
- **Prompt:**
  > Minimalist app icon, flat vector design. Abstract geometric logo representing connection, networks, and nodes forming a subtle, elegant 'R' letter. Clean intersecting paths, symmetric, professional. Color palette: warm rust and muted charcoal on a solid warm cream background (#FDF8F6). No shadows, no gradients, no text, clean vector art.

## 2. Splash Screen Icon (`splash-icon.png`)
- **File Path:** `assets/splash-icon.png`
- **Output:** 2048 × 2048 px, PNG (transparent background)
- **Prompt:**
  > Minimalist central logo graphic for a splash screen. An abstract geometric logo representing connection, matching the style of the app icon (clean intersecting paths/nodes), centered. Flat vector graphics. Solid color palette (muted charcoal and warm rust). Transparent background (no solid canvas), high resolution.

## 3. Android Adaptive Foreground (`android-icon-foreground.png`)
- **File Path:** `assets/android-icon-foreground.png`
- **Output:** 1024 × 1024 px, PNG (transparent background, logo safe zone centered)
- **Prompt:**
  > Android adaptive icon foreground layer. Simple, flat, bold geometric shape of the RelationOS connection logo centered inside the safe area. Minimalist vector shape. Transparent background, no background fill, single solid accent color (warm rust or muted charcoal).

## 4. Android Monochrome Icon (`android-icon-monochrome.png`)
- **File Path:** `assets/android-icon-monochrome.png`
- **Output:** 1024 × 1024 px, PNG (transparent background, flat solid black or dark grey logo)
- **Prompt:**
  > Android adaptive icon, monochromatic theme version. The flat, solid geometric connection logo from the RelationOS app icon. Clean silhouette, pure black graphic on a transparent background, no colors, no gradients, no grey tones.

## 5. Web Favicon (`favicon.png`)
- **File Path:** `assets/favicon.png`
- **Output:** 48 × 48 px, PNG (transparent background)
- **Prompt:**
  > Simplified favicon logo mark, 48x48 pixels. Abstract minimalist connection symbol, flat design, high contrast, clean shapes, transparent background.
